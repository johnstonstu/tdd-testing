Title:
Management wants info on the students

Narrative:
As a Manager I want to be able to search student list,
customize the results,
and apply analytics.

Scenarios:
1)
A teacher wants to see if a student is currently passing,
searches student by name,
sees if passing or failing

2)
A management wants to compile data of students based on age,
searches by range of age,
recieves list of students,
apply necessary data analytics.

2)
Sales wants to get overall satisfication of students,
searches list for satisfication
recieves satisfaction for all students,
apply necessary analytics.
